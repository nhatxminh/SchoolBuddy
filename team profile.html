3. Group process

Consider that communication is one of the core elements for the success of almost everything. Because of that, our team has put great effort into scheduling our meetup time, we organize our meeting about one to three meetings per week based on the density of each member's schedule, and if the assigned task is too complicated or hard to handle alone, that’s when our meeting volume will be adjusted to a higher rate. Each meeting will be about 1 and half an hour to 3 hours long. Our meeting will usually be hosted on Microsoft Team, due to its flexibility and convenience. We also communicate via Messenger, SMS message, or even call whenever a team’s individual needs help with the task. After each session, members will be assigned to a role, and we will all settle for a specific deadline. For the assigned tasks, our team always makes sure to finish the work before the deadline and publish their work onto Google Drive, where all our members can see and make any adjustments if needed. When a member couldn’t finish their work before the deadlines. If it’s their first time, then we will remind them, but if that ever occurs again, they will have to treat each team member with a drink of their choice.
